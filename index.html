CONTESTS

Here is why one needs to participate in contests regulary in platforms like CodeChef , CodeForces and Leetcode.....


Coding contests present a variety of challenging problems across different topics and difficulty levels. By regularly solving these problems, you enhance your problem-solving skills and become more proficient in algorithmic thinking.

Each contest introduces you to new and diverse problem types, ranging from classic algorithms to real-world applications. This exposure broadens your knowledge and prepares you to tackle a wide range of problems in competitions and real-world scenarios.

Competitive programming contests are time-bound, requiring you to solve problems efficiently within a limited timeframe. Regular participation helps you develop speed and accuracy in coding, enabling you to solve problems quickly and effectively under pressure.

Participating in contests allows you to engage with a vibrant community of programmers, exchange ideas, and learn from others' approaches and solutions. Collaborating with fellow participants through discussions and post-contest analysis can deepen your understanding of problem-solving techniques and foster a supportive learning environment.
